Deutsche in Italy

Deutsche introduces in Italy the expertise and knowledge acquired by a leading international group through innovative financial and management solutions intended for companies and individuals. Since the opening of the first branch in Milan in 1977, the Group has grown constantly, thanks also to the acquisitions of Banca d'America e d'Italia (1986), Banca Popolare di Lecco (1994) and of the financial promotion network Finanza & Futuro Banca (1995).
