    App "La Mia Banca"

    La Mia Banca is the official mobile app of Deutsche Italy for clients and db contocarta holders to allow access and transactions via smartphones and tablets.

    How to use "La Mia Banca"


    To start using La Mia Banca, you must activate the db Interactive Home Banking service and login using your credentials.

    If you have not subscribed yet to db Interactive, you can contact your branch or your financial advisor.
    If you have activated the service but you have lost your credentials, you can call Customer Service on (+39) 02.6995, option 2 or send an email to: db.interactive @ db.com.

    Main features of the app

    • Customized Home: customize the Home with the functions used frequently;
    • Your account: have a global overview of your accounts and investment products, check your account balance and list of transactions;
    • Cards: check the card balance and the latest movements. If you have a db Card World debit card, you can choose when to enable or disable it autonomously and in real time according to your needs, also enable and disable the online payment functionality for purchases made on the internet, select or deselect specific countries where the card can  be used and customizing the geographic area.
    • Money transfers: transfer money using your bank account or your db contocarta;
    • Payments: make payments on the go and check the status of your transactions within the Payment History. Thanks to La Mia Banca app, you can use the mobile camera to easily fill in postal bills;
    • My Documents: view communications related to your account;
    • Exchange and Markets: view major market indices, shares, exchange rates, interest rates, news and lots more
    • Send email: send the details of the transactions made via email;
    • Copy IBAN: access the list of transactions, see and send your IBAN in one tap;
    • Find us: find out branches, offices or ATMs nearby and how to reach them;
    • News and social: keep you updated with the latest news from financial markets and from our social media channels, YouTube, Twitter and Facebook for db Easy clients;
    • Contact us and FAQ: easily find phone numbers and useful information or ask for assistance.

    La Mia Banca is available in Italian, English and German.

    New! La Mia Banca app is now available also for Apple Watch

    Please download the app only from the following official stores: Apple Store and Google Play.
    Deutsche S.p.A. will not be responsible for any download outside the official app stores.


    Minimum device requirement:

    • Recommended hardware device configuration: 1GB RAM + 1GHz CPU
    • Minimum screen resolution: 480x800 pixels

    Would you like to help improve La Mia Banca or report any issue concerning the app?

    Write to us at info.lamiabanca@db.com.
