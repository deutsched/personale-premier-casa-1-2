Open Day 2014 | Fine art photography

September 4th, 2014 ? Milan

Guided tours to the Deutsche Collection in Turati venue, Milan

On Saturday, October 4th from 10am to 7pm, Deutsche will host the eight edition of the Open Day in its headquarter venue in via Turati, Milan. This event is part of "Invito a Palazzo" (invitation to the Palaces), the annual initiative promoted by ABI, the Italian Banking Association, for the banks in Italy to open their doors to the public.

The event, dedicated to "Fine art photography", will propose visitors a guided tour focused on Deutsche Collection's most important photographers. The Italian section of the Bank's contemporary art collection, hosted in Milan Bicocca Headquarters since 2007 and in Turati venue since 2012, is composed by more than 500 works by contemporary Italian and foreign artists, including young talents and outstanding exponents of the international art scene. This year's guided tours will include artists such as Vincenzo Castella, Luigi Ghirri, Günther Förg, Adrian Paci, Martin Liebscher and Candida Höfer.

We look forward to seeing you on Saturday, October 4th in Via Turati!
