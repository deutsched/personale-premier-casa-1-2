Launch of Made for Good in Italy

November 24th, 2016 ? Como

Fondazione Deutsche Italia has launched the global program Made for Good in Italy. Fondazione Deutsche Italia, in collaboration with Cometa will support the Workshop of Wood, the project dedicated to the young people that will get closer to the world of craftsmanship and Wood manufacturing.
