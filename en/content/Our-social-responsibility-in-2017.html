Our social responsibility in 2017

Download the brochure about the projects in favour of the Italian community

Fondazione Deutsche Italia is happy to present the brochure "La nostra responsabilità sociale nel 2017" (Our social responsibility in 2017), the annual publication about our projects for the Italian community and territory, where the Bank operates. In 2017, the Fondazione committed for € 478,000, supporting around 10,000 beneficiaries. These funds supported both national and local projects, mainly in the long run (from a three years duration on).

This publication collects last year's main projects in partnership projects with the best Italian non-profit organizations, developed within Deutsche Group's areas of intervention - young people (Born to Be program) and entrepreneurship for social good (Made for Good program) - with a clear focus on Italy and on education, being convinced that social progress in our country is based on merit and on talent recognition.

Alongside the projects dedicated to education, the Fondazione continued its general commitment to the disadvantaged and to foster a positive impact on the territory. Deutsche employees complete the picture and participate as corporate volunteers.

The projects supported by Fondazione Deutsche Italia are part of the Group's Corporate Responsibility program, which, in 2017, has reached 2.1 million of people, with the contribution of over 17,400 employees with more than 235.000 hours of corporate volunteering.
