Twitter Netiquette

Deutsche Italia Twitter account, @deutschebankIT, is managed by Deutsche Social Media Italy Team.

All tweets related to this account are created and released by the Team and refer mainly to the activities of the Bank in Italy. The account @deutschebankIT reserves the right to take part to the discussions with the users when related to news and information about Deutsche. Also, Deutsche reserves the right to delete from its list the followers who produce slandering, indecent, offensive and illegal contents, and report such tweets as spam. Moreover, the followers of the account @deutschebankIT do not automatically become following. In case of specific requests that do not concern directly the account @deutschebankIT, the users will be readdressed to and managed by the customer service system. The account @deutschebankIT is managed Monday to Friday, from 9am to 6pm and, only exceptionally, on Saturday, Sunday and holidays. To contact Deutsche Italia, look the section "Contacts" here provided.

Privacy

The security of your data cannot be guaranteed on public networks. We therefore suggest you to avoid using Twitter to send us confidential data and information, for which we invite you to use our Customer Service system; do not use passwords and codes similar to your checking account and to your home banking, bank card, credit card services etc.
