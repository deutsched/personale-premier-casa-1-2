We want to create sustainable value: for our clients and employees, our shareholders and for society. Our goal is clear: our high-performance culture must go hand-in-hand with a culture of responsibility. Through our economic success and our international presence, we aim to create value for our  shareholders, clients and, more in general for the society we live in.

We help young people to find their professional way through our Born to Be projects; we are with innovative entrepreneurs with the programme Made for good; we build opportunities In the Community also with the help of our Corporate Volunteers. We invest in Arts and Culture to make it accessible for everybody.
We respect the environment and we are constantly working to reduce the impact of our activities.

Since 2013, we operate through Deutsche Italy Foundation.

We invest in the communities and, hence, in our future.
