Deutsche Artist of the Year

The "Artist of the Year" award is dedicated to contemporary art. The award is meant to communicate new and exciting positions to the general public and promote promising international artists. Unlikely other awards, it is not only a monetary recognition, but Deutsche takes care of the organization of a temporary exhibition in different locations, the printing of catalogs and materials and the communication means as advertising.
