Fondazione Deutsche Italia

Fondazione Deutsche Italia is a vehicle through which the bank pursues its sustainability objectives. Born in 2013, the Foundation has collected and  with the objective to collect all the bank's resources for corporate citizenship. The Foundation has been working closely with important partners in not-for-profit world, in order to keep creating value for the society we live in.

the foundation is actively involved in three main fields:

  • Born to Be, helping young people to build their future;

  • Made for Good, supporting the innovative entrepreneurs;

  • In the Community, providing opportunities for the least fortunate.

 

 

Our projects

Since the beginning, Fondazione Deutsche Italia has been committed to support corporate citizenship initiatives. We work together many partners belonging to the not-for-profit world in order to improve life quality of many people in Italy.
